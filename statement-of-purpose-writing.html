What is the concept of Statement of Purpose?

The SOP is an essay cum application document that every student needs to write for enrollment in the international universities. You can even call it an admission essay, letter of motivation or simply SOP. It is a way through which one can introduce themselves, their qualities, experiences, career goal and passion towards the field. The main aim of the SOP is to come out through yours stories as the most deserving and meritorious candidate for the desired course.

Why is SOP so important?

In this competitive world everyone has some new ideas to bring to the table.it is quite a task to stand out among such large number of applicants with a stellar academic score. The admission panels are not in a position to understand the skill, interest pattern and the personality of the applicant solely from the academic results. However with a good statement of purpose, we are able to express our motivation, passion and career goals to them, making it convenient to look beyond just an academic score of an applicant.

Unlike any other document this document is completely under your control and it acts as the platform to express who you are and why you should be the part of the program. Through SOP you can share your vision and stand out among thousands of applicants. Hence SOP acts as that key that will help you getting admission despite not having a stellar academic performance as they take those who are fit for the course.

Here Are Our Expert Advice About How To Write A Perfect Statement Of Purpose

DO'S

  1. Smooth and grammatically correct sentences
  2. Academic background included
  3. Expression of recent and long term goals
  4. Usage of both active and passive voice.
  5. Be honest, consistent and straightforward.

DONT'S

  1. No exceeding word limits
  2. Avoid plagiarism
  3. Avoid mentioning interest in any other institute
  4. Don’t include unrealistic stories.
  5. Do not use same SOP for all.
  6. Don’t mention score of IELTS and GRE

There Are Some Common Mistakes Made By Students That Professional Writers Don’t:

  • Starting the SOP with phrase I’ve graduated from xyz high school with a GPA of xyz. This is one way that is bound to fail. Introduce yourself who you are, your goals and why you want to be part of their system.
